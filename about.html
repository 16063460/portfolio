One day I hope to become an employable concept artist for major media industries including cartoon shows or video games.

Despite being skilled at drawing my whole life, I am somewhat new to digital art and three dimensional animations. I have always had a strong interest in making two dimensional animations. I wish to have finally fully pursued my interest after I finish my degree here at Massey!

The source of my inspiration comes from the music that I listen to, artists that I look up to and themes that I find fascinating. My work is often emotionally expressive in some way. I use my drawings as a method of communication to the viewers the same way a writer would write a message for the readers. For me, my art is my most identifiable signifier of my personality.
